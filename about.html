about us

why choose us?

Ecoguard offer unforgettable wildlife experiences in South Sudan, combining adventure with education. Our expertly guided zoo visits and national park tours provide unique opportunities to connect with nature while supporting crucial conservation efforts.

Our educational programs are designed for all ages, fostering a deep understanding of wildlife and environmental protection. Every visit contributes to our mission of preserving biodiversity and enhancing conservation in South Sudan.
